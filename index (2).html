My Growth Over the Semester

  • I can play cleaner and smoother sounds with fewer squeaks
  • My breath control improved I can hold notes longer and stay steady
  • My hand placement is more accurate and consistent
  • My tonguing technique improved for clearer note beginnings
  • My confidence increased and I feel more comfortable performing in front of others

Conclusion

This class completely changed how I look at music I came in with no experience and now I understand how much focus and practice it takes to play even simple melodies

  • I learned the basics of music starting from nothing
  • I improved my breath control and tone quality
  • I can play smoother and cleaner sounds on the recorder
  • I developed better fingering accuracy and a clearer tonguing technique
  • My confidence grew a lot throughout the semester

Quick Facts

It is over 200 years old Sung by kids worldwide Perfect for bedtime
  • The words started as a poem in 1806 by Jane Taylor.
  • The tune came from a well known French melody.
  • It spread through schools and homes and became popular.

Why People Love It

  • It is easy to remember.
  • The melody is smooth and gentle.
  • It helps children relax.

Random Facts

  • The tune is shared with some ABC versions and Baa Baa Black Sheep.
  • Mozart wrote variations of this melody when he was young.
  • The words are English but the melody is French.
